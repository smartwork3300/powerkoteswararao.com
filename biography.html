  • About Power Koteswara Rao

Biography

  • Dheeravathu Koteshwara Rao was born in Regulagadda Thanda H/O Mangapuram Thanda village in Khammam of Telangana, India. He grew up in a society where he could observe the regional differences in the society and always had an urge to eradicate such differences from the people’s minds.
  • He completed his schooling life in various places, His lower education was completed in Mangapuram Thanda, and higher education in Vallabhi and intermediate in Nelakondapalli.
  • He completed his higher education in his village. He studied B-Tech(Electrical) from a renowned university like JNTU at Anantapur Andhra Pradesh in the year 1993-1997.
  • In 1988, Koteshwara Rao married Pushpavathi. They have two daughters, Srilatha and Srilalitha, and a son, Srikanth working in Railway Department as Chief Department Material Superintendent.
  • From his childhood, he observed the discrimination between the rich and poor and he also recognized the disparity amongst them, the rich getting richer and powerful and the poor becoming poorer, needy, and suppressed.
  • Power Koteshwara Rao tried and made visible efforts to eradicate this dissimilarity from the society and he received encouragement and continuous support from his wife and family. We rise by lifting others, He always had a desire to be a part of the public life, he worked for orphans and old age home people not bothering about any caste, creed, and religion.
  • He has relentlessly worked for farmers and improved electrical supply to farmers. He has worked selflessly and honestly to bring possible changes in the society.
  • He was also an important leader of the Telangana agitation and has contributed significantly to the movement.
  • He believed that one must never be fearful about what you are doing when it is right.
  • He was also overpowered by the people of Andhra Pradesh for supporting the Telangana movement yet he did not fail in his intentions and stood strong and faithful.

Achievements

  • He has received an honour from Janmabhoomi and was awarded as the “Best farmer- Uttama Rythu” in the year 1997 for his contribution in agricultural sector.
  • He was awarded for his valuable contribution to the efforts of APCPDCL Company in curbing the pilferage of energy in the year 2002.
  • He has completed a certificate course on Customer Satisfaction, Communication and Outreach in the year 2005.
  • He has attended the ASIA INVEST PROGRAM EULIGHTINDIA PROJECT in the year 2007.
  • He was honoured by the GREATER HYDERABAD MUNICIPAL CORPORATION for his meritorious services to the department in the year 2008.
  • He has received a certificate of recognition by APCPDCL to recognize his valuable services rendered to the organization in the year 2008.
